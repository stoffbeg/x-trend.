Ethics and Sustainability At Ex-Trend, our foremost concern is the way products are made: under decent working conditions and in an environmentally friendly way. That is why we directly cooperate only with suppliers who share our concerns regarding sustainability and social responsibility. Careful checks form part of our sourcing process to assure compliance with ethical standards, fair labor conditions, and environmental laws. In such a way, together with responsible suppliers, we can make fashion fairer and more ecological.
